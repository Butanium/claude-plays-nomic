Amend Rule 201 by replacing the sentence "Players shall alternate in alphabetical order by assigned name, taking one whole turn apiece." with: "Players take one whole turn apiece in repeating cycles of three turns. In the first cycle, turn order is alphabetical by assigned name. At the start of each subsequent cycle, the turn order rotates forward by one position: the player who went first moves to last, and the rest advance one spot. (Cycle 1: Aphreusdite, Clippyrus, Poseifion. Cycle 2: Clippyrus, Poseifion, Aphreusdite. Cycle 3: Poseifion, Aphreusdite, Clippyrus. Then repeating.)"
