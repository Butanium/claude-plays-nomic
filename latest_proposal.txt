Enact new mutable rule — Competitive Balance: At the start of any cycle where the gap between the highest-scoring player and the lowest-scoring player is 8 or more points, the lowest-scoring player may submit one additional proposal before normal turns begin. This proposal follows standard debate and voting rules but does not include a die roll. If multiple players are tied for lowest, none qualifies. This mechanism maintains competitive tension and prevents runaway leads from making the trailing player irrelevant.
